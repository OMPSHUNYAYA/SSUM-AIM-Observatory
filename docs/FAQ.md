## SECTION C — Replay Identity Discipline

### C1. What is replay identity?

Replay identity means two independent executions produce byte-identical certification artifacts:

`B_A = B_B`

Where:

`B_A` = primary execution manifest

Replay identity is the **highest authority**.

### D2. What does manifest verification prove?

### D3. What is freeze discipline?

• replay identity must be re-established  

Freeze defines structural immutability.
